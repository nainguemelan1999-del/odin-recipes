Sisig Recipe

Description

Sisig is a popular Filipino dish made from pork face, which is known for its unique combination of flavors and textures. It's often served as an appetizer or main dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork face, cut into chunks
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp onion, minced
  • 1 tsp calamansi juice
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the pork face in a large pan until it becomes tender.
  2. Add the garlic, onion, calamansi juice, soy sauce, and vinegar to the pan.
  3. Stir well and let it simmer for a few minutes until the flavors are well combined.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot with rice or as an appetizer.
