Sinigang Recipe

Description

Sinigang is a popular Filipino sour soup known for its tangy and savory flavors. It's typically made with a variety of vegetables and meat, creating a hearty and satisfying dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork shoulder, cut into chunks
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup tamarind soup
  • 1 cup vegetables (e.g., okra, eggplant, tomatoes)
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp onion, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large pot over medium heat and add the pork shoulder.
  2. Cook until the pork is browned on all sides.
  3. Add the water and tamarind soup to the pot.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 1 hour or until the pork is tender.
  5. Add the vegetables, garlic, and onion to the pot.
  6. Cook for another 10-15 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice.
